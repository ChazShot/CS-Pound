Invite!

Archive

Search through the ChickenSmoothie archives for a specific species or artist from a particular year.

Fair

Determines whether the given trade is fair based off of general trading rules such as 3 month rule and rarity.

Oekaki

Displays Oekaki drawing of given link, along with stats and the user who submitted it.

Pet

Displays all pet information from the given link; Owner, pet ID, name etc...

Time

Sends back a message telling you how long until the pound opens.

Trade

Displays trade information from the given link, the pets/items/CS on each side.
